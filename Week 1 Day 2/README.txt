Styling Summary:
Font: Arial, sans-serif for good looking text.
Layout: Centered text and grid-based gallery.
Colors: Light gray background, blue boxes with white text.
Effects: Rounded image corners and shadows.
Flexbox: Used for flexible content arrangement.

Responsive Design:
Tablets (≤768px): The gallery switches to two columns, and the heading size reduces.
Mobile (≤480px): The gallery becomes a single column, text shrinks, and images resize.
Flexbox Adjustments: Content stacks in smaller screens for better visibility and usability.
